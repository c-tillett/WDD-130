FIREBALL

What is FIREBALL?

FIREBALL is a weekly Fighting Game Tournament Series located in Rexburg Idaho. In Rexburg, tournaments and organizations surrounding Super Smash Brothers have always been prevelant. Though some have tried, many have failed to give traditional Fighting Games a true home in Rexburg. Upon the release of Street Fighter 6, however, something shook up the smash community. Interest in traditional, or 2-Demensional Fighting games took flight. Since January of 2023, FIREBALL has been a weekly event, allowing those who put in the effort to show their dominance in Street Fighter VI, Guilty Gear: Strive, Tekken 8, and, more recently, Marvel vs. Capcom 2. All are welcome at FIREBALL! If you've never played any of the games that we run, that's okay! We'd love to teach you. You haven't played in a long time? Welcome back, we'll help de-rust those skills of yours! Don't have a controller? We have spares! I play on Xbox, but FIREBALL is ran largely on PS5's. I have an adapter that will work for you! Questions here left unanswered? Join our Discord server by clicking on the button in the top-right! Take a look at our Organizers page to meet the faces that make sure FIREBALL runs smoothly every week, and take a look at the History page to view past results, brackets, and videos from FIREBALL.

Tournament Organizers

Nervous

Nervous is the head tournament organizer for Fireball. After discovering Street Fighter in November of 2023, he broke away from the Smash community, and put together the FIREBALL tournament series. Loves Terry Bogard.

BackrouncVoice

BackroundVoice is one of the backbones of Fireball. Equipped with extensive knowledge of the FGC beyond the confines of Rexburg, and a well equipped setup, he offers a lot to the community. Professional hugger (grappler).

Elucid

Why this dude is here competing with us is a mystery that few can solve. Placed 25th at Evolution 2023 in Guilty Gear: Strive. His 3000+ hours of Guilty Gear allows a lot of newer players to learn a lot. That, alongside his experience TOing has allowed the Fireball Series to expand beyond anything that it could've otherwise. Will not approach.

2%

Venue expert. He allows us to set up in the lounge where he lives, and makes sure that we can all get in. Combined with his artistic talent (bringing us our image on the homepage), he brings things unreplacable to this small scene. Has fundamentals so good, that he has gotten into our top 5 Street Fighter players without even owning the game.

Slimey274

He may be new to the FGC as a whole, but his fire for learning and improvement is something that all should aspire to. Brings a positive energy that can't be replaced. Best popoffs.

Blazelighter

What he may lack in first place finishes, he makes up for in passion and knowledge of frame data. Ask him anything about Guilty Gear, and he'll have an answer. Brings the mic so that our audio can be clear on stream. Needs a new controller.

YummyBoii

Few will be able to name a character more jovial than YummyBoii. Equiped with years of experience, a setup loaded with games that few have even heard of before, killer trash talk, good advice, and solid results to boot, YummyBoii is irreplacable in this community.

Zortos

This certified oldhead loves to bring the hands in Street Fighter. His fundamentals and game-sense carry him in our other events. He trives off of hitting the Iron Man infinite in Marvel vs Capcom 2, getting Perfect KO's, simple combo routes, and, above all else, spreading his knowledge to newer players.

Ques0

Because this guy decided to buy Street Fighter VI and teach Nervous some basics, FIREBALL has come to be. Time and time again, he proves that he has the means to stand at the peak with other top players. His respect for others and insatiable appatiete for improvement keeps players better than him around, raising the overall quality of our scene
