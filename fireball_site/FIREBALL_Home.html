This is FIREBALL

The home of the FGC in Rexburg, ID

FIREBALL is a weekly Fighting Game Tournament Series located in Rexburg Idaho. In Rexburg, tournaments and organizations surrounding Super Smash Brothers have always been prevelant. Though some have tried, many have failed to give traditional Fighting Games a true home in Rexburg. Upon the release of Street Fighter 6, however, something shook up the smash community. Interest in traditional, or 2-Demensional Fighting games took flight. Since January of 2023, FIREBALL has been a weekly event, allowing those who put in the effort to show their dominance in Street Fighter VI, Guilty Gear: Strive, Tekken 8, and, more recently, Marvel vs. Capcom 2. All are welcome at FIREBALL! If you've never played any of the games that we run, that's okay! We'd love to teach you. You haven't played in a long time? Welcome back, we'll help de-rust those skills of yours! Don't have a controller? We have spares! I play on Xbox, but FIREBALL is ran largely on PS5's. I have an adapter that will work for you! Questions here left unanswered? Join our Discord server by clicking on the button in the top-right!
